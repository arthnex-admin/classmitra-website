How to Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your ClassMitra account and all associated data at any time. We've made this process simple and transparent.

Account deletion and data removal policy
On this page
1. Delete from the app 2. Request via email 3. Request via WhatsApp 4. What happens next 5. Important to know 6. Confirmation 7. Questions

1Method 1 — Delete from inside the app

In-app deletion
Fastest
  1. Open the ClassMitra app on your phone
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom right of the screen
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the profile screen
  4. Tap "Delete My Account"
  5. You will be asked to confirm your decision — read the warning carefully
  6. Enter your registered mobile number to confirm your identity
  7. Tap "Yes, Delete My Account Permanently"
  8. Your account deletion request is submitted immediately

You will receive a confirmation WhatsApp message and push notification within a few minutes confirming that your deletion request has been received.

2Method 2 — Request via email

If you are unable to access the app or prefer to request deletion by email:

Email request
7 business days
  1. Send an email to hello@classmitra.in
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request — ClassMitra"
  3. Include in the email: your registered mobile number, your full name as registered on ClassMitra, your role (Teacher or Parent), and optionally a reason for deletion (helps us improve)
  4. We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

3Method 3 — Request via WhatsApp

WhatsApp request
7 business days

Send a WhatsApp message to +91 98765 00000 with the message:

"Delete my ClassMitra account — [your registered mobile number]"

Our support team will verify and process your request within 7 business days.

4What happens when you delete your account

If you are a Teacher:

Data What happens
Teacher profile (name, photo, subjects, area) Permanently deleted within 30 days
All batch information Permanently deleted within 30 days
All student records Permanently deleted within 30 days
All homework posts and photos Permanently deleted within 30 days
All attendance records Permanently deleted within 30 days
All fees records Permanently deleted within 30 days
All notices and announcements Permanently deleted within 30 days
Parent accounts linked to your class Remain active but show no class data
Subscription billing Cancelled immediately — no further charges
Active subscription period Remains active until end of billing cycle — no refund for unused days

If you are a Parent:

Data What happens
Parent profile (name, phone number) Permanently deleted within 30 days
FCM notification token Deleted immediately — you will stop receiving notifications
Homework and class data (belongs to teacher's class) Anonymised — your name is removed but class records remain for the teacher
Fees payment records Anonymised — teacher retains fee records without your personal details

5Important things to know before deleting

Deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All your data will be gone.
  • Teachers: Deleting your account will remove access for all parents connected to your classes. Inform your parents before deleting.
  • Active subscription: If you are a teacher with an active paid subscription, your subscription will be cancelled upon deletion. You will not be charged again, but the current period's payment is non-refundable.
  • Backup your data first: If you need to keep any records (student list, fees history, attendance records), please export or screenshot them before requesting deletion. We cannot recover data after deletion is complete.
  • Processing time: Account deletion is completed within 30 days of your confirmed request. Backup copies are purged within 60 days.
  • Legal hold: In rare cases where your account is subject to a legal investigation or court order, we may be required to retain certain data for a legally mandated period even after your deletion request.

6Confirmation of deletion

Once your account has been fully deleted, you will receive:

  • A final confirmation WhatsApp message to your registered number
  • A confirmation email (if you provided one during the deletion request)
After this confirmation, your data is permanently removed from all ClassMitra systems and backups within 60 days.

7Questions about account deletion

If you have any questions about account deletion or data removal, contact us. We aim to respond to all account deletion queries within 24 hours during support hours.
