2Description of Service

ClassMitra is a mobile application designed to bridge the communication gap between tuition teachers and parents of students (primarily KG to 8th standard). The Platform provides the following core services:

  • Homework posting and delivery (text and photo-based)
  • Attendance marking and tracking
  • Tuition fees tracking and digital receipt generation
  • Class notices and announcements
  • Student progress tracking
  • Push notifications and WhatsApp-based communication alerts
  • Student and batch management for tuition teachers

The primary functionality is delivered through Android and iOS mobile applications. A supporting website at classmitra.in provides information about the service.
