Privacy Policy

How ClassMitra collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information — in plain language, compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023.

Last updated: June 2025

1Introduction

Arthnex Fintech Pvt Ltd ("ClassMitra", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use the ClassMitra mobile application and website (classmitra.in).

This policy complies with the Information Technology Act 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act).

By using ClassMitra, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the platform.

2Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the ClassMitra service.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier
  • App usage data: Screens visited, features used, session duration — used only for improving the app
  • FCM token: Firebase Cloud Messaging token used solely for sending push notifications to your device
  • IP address: Collected by Firebase for security purposes
  • Crash logs: App crash reports used for debugging and improving stability

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect precise GPS location, access your contacts/camera/microphone without explicit permission, collect financial account details (handled by Razorpay), collect biometric data, track your activity on other apps or websites, or build advertising profiles.

3How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service: Delivering homework to parents, marking attendance, tracking fees, sending notices
  • Authentication: Verifying your identity via phone OTP
  • Push notifications: Sending homework alerts, fee reminders, absence notifications, and class notices to parents
  • WhatsApp notifications: Sending automated homework summaries and alerts via WhatsApp (opt-in only)
  • Customer support: Responding to queries submitted through the contact form or WhatsApp
  • App improvement: Analysing aggregated, anonymised usage data to improve features
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable Indian laws and court orders
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell your data, and we do not share your data with marketing companies.

4Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

All ClassMitra data is stored on Google Firebase, which operates on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure. Firebase data centres serving Indian users are located in Asia (Singapore and Mumbai regions). Your data is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS)
  • All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption by Firebase
  • Firebase Authentication handles all password and OTP verification — we never store raw passwords
  • Access to the Firestore database is controlled by strict security rules — teachers can only access their own class data, parents can only access their child's data
  • Administrative access to the Firebase console is protected by multi-factor authentication
  • We conduct regular security reviews of our Firestore security rules

4.3 Data Retention

Scenario Retention

Teacher deletes account All student, homework, attendance & fees data permanently deleted within 30 days
Parent deletes account Personal profile deleted within 30 days; homework/attendance records anonymised
Backup data Retained up to 60 days post-deletion for disaster recovery, then destroyed
Contact form submissions Retained for 1 year for support reference

5Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

5.1 Within the ClassMitra Platform

  • Teacher's homework, notices, and attendance records are shared with parents of students in that teacher's class — this is the core function of the service
  • A teacher can see the names and contact details of their own students and parents only

5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

Provider What they receive
Google Firebase Stores all app data, handles authentication, sends push notifications
Twilio / Meta WhatsApp Business API Parent phone numbers, only with opt-in consent, to deliver WhatsApp notifications
Razorpay Payment-relevant data only — not your app data
Google Play / Apple App Store No personal data beyond what's required for app publishing

All third-party providers are contractually bound to use your data only for the specific purpose for which it was shared and to maintain appropriate security standards.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by a valid court order, law enforcement request, or other legal process under applicable Indian law. We will notify affected users of such requests where legally permitted to do so.

5.4 Business Transfer

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of Arthnex Fintech Pvt Ltd, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify users via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before any such transfer.

We never sell your personal data. We never share your data with advertisers.

6WhatsApp Notifications

ClassMitra sends WhatsApp messages to parents as a supplementary notification channel. This works as follows:

  1. A teacher adds a parent's phone number when creating a student profile
  2. The parent receives a one-time WhatsApp invite message from ClassMitra's official WhatsApp Business number
  3. By joining ClassMitra, parents opt-in to receiving homework and class notifications via WhatsApp
  4. Parents can opt out of WhatsApp notifications at any time through their app profile settings, or by replying STOP to any ClassMitra WhatsApp message

We only send transactional messages (homework, attendance, fees, notices) — we do not send promotional or marketing messages via WhatsApp.

7Children's Privacy

ClassMitra serves students as young as KG (approximately 3 years old). We take the privacy of children very seriously.
  • Student accounts are managed by their teachers and parents — children do not directly create accounts or provide personal data to ClassMitra
  • We collect only the minimum student information required: name and standard (grade level)
  • We do not collect photos, biometric data, or location data of students
  • We do not display student data publicly
  • Parents have full rights to access, correct, and delete their child's data by contacting hello@classmitra.in
  • ClassMitra complies with the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act (POCSO) 2012 and any relevant provisions of the DPDP Act 2023 regarding children's data

8Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (India), you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to grievance redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer if you believe your data has been mishandled
  • Right to nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise your rights on your behalf in case of incapacity

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at hello@classmitra.in. We will respond to all valid data requests within 30 days.

9Cookies and Tracking

The ClassMitra mobile app does not use cookies. The ClassMitra website (classmitra.in) uses only essential technical cookies required for the website to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that identify individual users. We use anonymised, aggregated Google Analytics data to understand overall website traffic.

10Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via push notification or by displaying a prominent notice within the app at least 14 days before changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy reflects the most recent revision. Your continued use of ClassMitra after the effective date of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
